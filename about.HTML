About the Company

The company is focused on tackling urban traffic and parking congestion using smart, practical solutions.

Advantages of Traffic Congestion

  1. Encourages the use of public transport and carpooling

Disadvantages of Traffic Congestion

  1. Causes time delays and increased fuel consumption
  2. Raises pollution and stress levels

Advantages of Parking Congestion

  1. Provides convenience and supports local businesses
  2. Improves safety and organization

Disadvantages of Parking Congestion

  1. Takes up valuable land and can be costly to build
  2. Encourages more car use, worsening congestion
  3. Searching for parking adds to traffic problems
